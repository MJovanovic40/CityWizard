The nearby settlement of Devojački Bunar is also officially part of Banatski Karlovac.Banatski Karlovac (Serbian Cyrillic: Банатски Карловац) is a town located in the Alibunar Municipality, in the South Banat District of Serbia.It is situated in the Autonomous Province of Vojvodina.The town has a Serb ethnic majority and its population numbers 5,820 people (2002 census).The nearby settlement of Devojački Bunar is also officially part of Banatski Karlovac.
