Plandište (Serbian Cyrillic: Пландиште, pronounced [plândiːʃte]), also known as Zichydorf, Zichyfavla, or Mariolana is a village and municipality located in the South Banat District of the autonomous province of Vojvodina, Serbia. The village has a population of 3,832, while Plandište municipality has 11,336 inhabitants. One of municipality's best known landmarks is Kapetanovo Castle, located in Stari Lec.
