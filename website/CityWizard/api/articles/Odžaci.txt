Odžaci (Serbian Cyrillic: Оџаци, pronounced [ˈǒdʒat͜si]) is a town and municipality located in the West Bačka District of the autonomous province of Vojvodina, Serbia. The town of Odžaci has a population of 8,795 people, while the population of the municipality of Odžaci is 30,196 people (2011 census).
